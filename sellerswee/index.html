7 steps to grow your business with WEE

01
Fill in the request form to join the WEE service

You are a UAE business owner with a valid trade license? We will answer all your questions and help to complete the registration.

02
Adding products and points of sale

You create product cards in your personal account. Product availability at your points of sale can be loaded via API, manually or using a file.

03
We attract new buyers

WEE brings in traffic from different internet online marketing channels. More than 72% of customers are new to our partners. We will also be able to run promotions to attract even more customers to your store

04
You only receive prepaid orders

Customers see offers from the delivery area where sellers are located, add necessary products to the cart and prepay the order. You don’t need to contact the customer and solve any issues.

05
Process the order within 15 min

You receive an order with a list of products to pack and hand to our courier within 15 min.

06
We deliver the products within an hour

The courier collects the order and delivers it to the customer. This way, the customer receives the order within one hour of placing it.

07
You receive secure payments at least once every 14 days

We do not charge for traffic, commission is charged only for completed orders. WEE processes all payments. Funds for the orders are transferred to your bank account at least once every two weeks.

FAQ

How many SKUs can I add to the marketplace?

We don’t place any restrictions on the range, product analysts only offer advice.

What is your commission?

The commission depends on the category of products and starts at 5% (only completed orders). The full list of fees and commissions can be found here.

What time can a customer place an order?

Business hours of all points of sale should be mentioned by sellers in their personal account. The buyer will be able to view offers of the store and add products to cart any time 24/7. But they will not be able to place an order till the business hours of the store begin and will receive an additional notification.
